# Navigation

Navigation follows intent first.

Customers arrive with human goals:

- I want to play.
- I want to meet people.
- I want to book.
- I want to belong.

Inventory may exist when browsing is the intent, but internal product structure should never be forced onto a customer who arrived with a human goal.

The source of truth is Article IX in the [Product Constitution](../constitution/Product%20Constitution.md).
