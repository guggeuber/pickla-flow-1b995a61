# Constitution Court

The Court resolves constitutional conflicts.

It should stay small. It is not a meeting format for every decision. Most questions should die in the hallway test.

## Standing

Anyone may raise a constitutional objection by citing an article:

- Engineer
- Designer
- Desk staff
- Franchisee
- Founder
- Agent-flagged review

Raising an objection is free and can never be held against the raiser.

## Ladder

1. **Hallway test:** What would a great host do?
2. **Reality:** if the question is reversible and measurable, run the experiment. Reality has veto power only through the Constitutional Metrics.
3. **Product Court:** for irreversible or dignity questions. Composition: Experience Review, Architecture Review, and one rotating operator seat. The founder holds veto.
4. **Precedent:** every ruling and founder veto is written into git: question, articles cited, ruling, reasoning.

## Amendment

Any article may be amended by the Court with founder ratification.

The twenty-article cap is itself amendable only by unanimous Court plus founder.

The purpose is not process.

The purpose is to prevent quiet workarounds from becoming the real constitution.
